The Studio

We're building games for the long run.

Nitro Sloth Games is an independent game studio creating polished interactive experiences while investing in a brand that will grow for years to come.

Our Story

Nitro Sloth Games was founded on a simple insight: the game industry moves at breakneck speeds, but building memorable experiences takes focus, planning, and patience.

We established this studio not to chase fleeting hypercasual trends, but to build a robust, scalable structure that supports games spanning multiple genres over the next decade.

"Sloth energy. Nitro speed."

Sloth energy represents our dedication to careful planning, clean architecture, and balanced, healthy work habits. Nitro speed represents our ability to execute quickly, push updates, and adapt to player feedback immediately once the plan is set.

Meet the Jetpack Sloth - our official studio mascot!

Our Mission

To create polished, memorable interactive experiences that delight players while building a sustainable independent studio capable of long-term innovation. We measure success by player satisfaction and the consistency of our releases.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized independent game studio whose name represents quality, creativity, and trust across entertainment, education, and business interactive experiences.
