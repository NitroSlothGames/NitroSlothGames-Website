About Nitro Sloth Games

Nitro Sloth Games is an independent game development and publishing studio based in India, operating as a venture under Klyph OPC. The studio focuses on creating highly polished mobile game mechanics that are quick to learn, addictive to play, and accessible to everyone.

Founded by Codex (programming/Unity), Shyam (creative/art), and Hiresh (growth/UA), the studio's tagline is "Sloth energy, Nitro speed." This represents the team's commitment to balanced, structured planning combined with rapid, agile execution.
