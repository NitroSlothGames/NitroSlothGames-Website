Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 8, 2026

At Nitro Sloth Games (a branded venture operating under Klyph OPC), accessible from our website and our mobile applications published on the Google Play Store, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors and players. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Nitro Sloth Games and how we use it.

1. General Information & Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to our website and our mobile games (including, but not limited to, Crayon Sorter). By downloading our games or browsing our site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect personal identifying information (PII) such as your name, residential address, telephone number, or billing credentials unless you voluntarily provide them to us (for example, by sending us a direct support inquiry via email).

3. Automated Data Collection & Analytics

Our mobile games use third-party libraries that automatically collect anonymous metadata to help us analyze and improve our services:

  • Firebase Analytics: We track anonymous gameplay events (such as levels completed, average session length, and button clicks) to adjust level designs and optimize UI flows.
  • Device Specifications: We collect hardware configurations (OS version, device model, screen resolution) to troubleshoot bugs and crashes.

4. Advertising Partners (AdMob)

To support our studio, our free-to-play mobile games display ads served via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data (such as advertising IDs or cookies) to personalize advertisements based on your interests or geographical location. You can opt-out of personalized ads under your mobile device's settings.

5. Contacting Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, or would like to request clarification regarding anonymous log data, please contact us at nitrosloth.hq@gmail.com.
